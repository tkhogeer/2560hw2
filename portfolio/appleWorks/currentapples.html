Our Current Apples

GoldRush

GoldRush has a lively flavor, tart off the tree, becomes sweeter upon storage while maintaining sprightliness and crunch, all purpose.

Suncrisp

Suncrisp is pleasantly but not overwhelmingly tart, sweet enough to appeal to a wide audience, good for eating, sauces, pies, cider and baking.

Braeburn

Braeburn is richly sweet/tart, very crisp and juicy, good for eating, pies, baking, sauces and salads.

Sundance

Sundance is quite crisp with a sharp flavor that mellows to sweet/tart upon storage, excellent dessert apple.

Fuji

Fuji is sweet off the tree, growing sweeter and richer in storage, keeps crisp and juicy up to 8 months, good for eating and salads.

Pink Lady

Pink Lady is zesty tart/sweet, good for eating, baking, pies, salads and sauces.

Yellow Delicious

Yellow Delicious has long been a sweet favorite, versatile apple, stays chunky when cooked, good for eating, sauces, pies, baking, salads, freezing slices, drying.

Stayman Winesap

Stayman Winesap is tart, crisp and juicy with winelike flavor, good for eating, salads, pies and baking.

Arkansas Black

Arkansas Black is quite tannic with aromas of dry wine and hickory bark, mellows in storage, good for eating, salads and cider.

Red Delicious

Red Delicious is an old favorite with distinctive sweet flavor, we grow Red Chief strain, good for eating.
